  2. Normalization Formula

  Training (normalize):
  normalized = (grs - 6) / 24
  Example: GRS=15 вҶ’ normalized = (15-6)/24 = 0.375

  Inference (denormalize):
  score = (normalized - 0) / 1 * (max - min) + min
  - For [1, 10]: score = normalized * 9 + 1
  - For [6, 30]: score = normalized * 24 + 6
